The Bishop

Dr. Patrick Elijah Anyomi is the warm and dynamic founding Bishop and General Director of Missions of The Continental Charismatic Evangelistic Missions (COCEM) and The World Missionary Church International with its Headquarters in Accra, Ghana. Today, the ministry of Dr. Patrick Elijah Anyomi includes, Pastoring The headquarters Of the World Missionary Church International, Redco Accra Ghana; Missions and Church Planting locally and internationally, The World Missions University College, where he serves as president.

He is an author of several Books. Dr Anyomi is a favorite speaker in many Church Conferences in Ghana and around the world. He also shares the Gospel frequently on Anyomi TV on Facebook and YouTube. Bishop Anyomi also serves on many national and international Christian bodies, like the National Association of Charismatic and Christian Churches (NACCC) Ghana and the International Communion of Charismatic Churches (ICCC), USA. Dr. Anyomi is an alumnus of the University of Ghana Business School, Legon and holds a Doctor of Religious Philosophy Degree from the Evangel Christian University of America (ECUA).

He lives in Accra, Ghana, West Africa with his wife Lynda and Has three children; Rev. Sarah Sweetie, Rev. Prince David and Dr. Daisy Deborah.

Our Mission

We're committed to going into the highways and byways preaching Jesus and making disciples in every town, village and city in Ghana and worldwide. We are missionaries after the Acts of Apostles, men and women led by the Spirit.

"I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the Lord." (Psalm 122)

The World Missions Bible College is located on a hill at Legonman Estates (Redco) East Adentan, in the GA East District of the Greater Accra Region, Ghana. The college was set up in 1991 with the goal of training missionary leadership of all the five fold calling of Ephesians 4: 11 after the Manner and purpose for which the Lord called the twelve. “He appointed twelve …… that they might be with him and that he might send them out to preach or to have authority over demons [Mark 3:14,15] Hence the emphasis of the programmes offered is to impart practical skills with knowledge. Thus in addition to academic work there are practical training periods on the Mission field..
